### Biography
 
| <br>I am a postdoctoral research fellow at the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, [Nanyang Technological University](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/), Singapore, working with [Prof. Bihan Wen](https://personal.ntu.edu.sg/bihan.wen/). Prior to that, I was a postdoctoral research fellow at the School of Information and Communication Engineering, [University of Electronic Science and Technology of China](https://www.uestc.edu.cn/), Chengdu, China, working with [Prof. Ce Zhu](http://www.avc2-lab.net/~eczhu/). I received a Ph.D. degree from the School of Electronic Science and Engineering, [Nanjing University](https://www.nju.edu.cn/main.htm), Nanjing, China, in 2018. My research interests include inverse problems in image and volume data (e.g., video, Hyperspectral image and medical image) processing, sparse signal representation, and machine learning. <br> <br> **Email：** <zhazhiyuan.mmd@gmail.com>, <zhiyuan.zha@ntu.edu.sg>. <br><br>|  ![image](https://zhazhiyuan.github.io/images/IMG_2698.png)|
